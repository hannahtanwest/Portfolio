How Amentes Came to Be

Amentes was created to fill a gap — a space where queerness is simply a part of you, not your whole identity. It's a space where you can feel seen for who you are without needing to explain yourself. Born from a realization that Copenhagen needed more queer and FLINTA-centered events, Amentes aims to build a genuine community — not overnight, but slowly and authentically. It's a place for people like us, created from a need to belong.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to become a trusted name in the queer community — not as a trendy “queer brand,” but as a space where creativity, connection, and authenticity thrive. Like SoHo House empowers its members, Amentes aims to uplift queer talent and create spaces where people feel at home. We believe in:

  • Inclusivity – Everyone deserves to feel seen.
  • Creativity – A platform for queer voices and artistry.
  • Authenticity – A space made by queer BIPOC, for queer BIPOC.
  • Empowerment – We want to lift each other up.

Tone & Personality

Our voice is empowering, fun, and unapologetically inclusive. We speak to our audience like a trusted friend — bold but never aggressive, playful but never careless.

“For Unconventional Lovers.”

This tagline captures the heart of Amentes — queerness is still seen as unconventional, but here, we're rewriting that narrative.

Core Offerings

Amentes curates experiences designed for queer connection and creativity:

  • Parties – High-energy, safe, and inclusive.
  • Creative Workshops – Spaces to explore and create.
  • Live Music Nights – Showcasing queer talent.
  • Speed Dating & Game Nights – Building real connections.
  • Community Spaces – A place to gather and feel at home.
