MUSE

Studio

Multimedia Design

For my Multimedia Design exam, I helped create a fully coded website for Muse Studio, our fictional marketing company designed to assist businesses with their digital marketing needs. The project was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js, focusing on providing a seamless and professional user experience. Væleur Studio served as our test client, allowing us to simulate the real-world process of handling customer requirements and delivering tailored solutions.

The Main Focus

The project involved developing a responsive and user-friendly website, ensuring a clean and modern design that aligned with Væleur Studio's brand identity. We placed a strong emphasis on creating an intuitive user experience (UI/UX) to maximize customer engagement and retention. In addition to the technical aspects, we also developed a comprehensive marketing strategy, including social media and content recommendations, to help Væleur Studio increase its online presence. Simulating real-world client collaboration allowed us to refine the product based on feedback, ensuring that both functionality and user experience were prioritized.
