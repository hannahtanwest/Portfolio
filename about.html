About

Hi, My Name Is Hannah!

I'm a Web Development student at KEA with an AP in Multimedia Design from CPH Business, specializing in Frontend Development. I'm passionate about blending artistic expression with technical expertise to create visually appealing and functional digital experiences.

In addition to my studies, I run my own company, Amentes, which hosts events for the FLINTA queer community in Copenhagen. This venture allows me to combine my creative skills with project management and strategic planning—developing stunning visuals, organizing events, and executing projects from concept to completion. The experience of leading Amentes directly enhances my work as a web developer, reinforcing my ability to manage complex projects and deliver engaging user experiences.

On a personal note, I enjoy playing guitar and piano, and I find cooking to be both therapeutic and a meaningful way to connect with others. Building and nurturing relationships is a core value in my life, and I take pride in fostering strong connections both personally and professionally.
